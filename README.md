## About Me

I'm an ML engineer and designer based in **Taipei, Taiwan** 🇹🇼, focused on making AI tools accessible to Traditional Chinese speakers through open-source contributions, translation, and community building.

I contribute to the **Hugging Face** ecosystem (Transformers, Optimum-Quanto, HF Course) and the **[marimo](https://marimo.io)** reactive notebook project. I'm also a core member and brand designer of **[Twinkle AI](https://huggingface.co/twinkle-ai)** — Taiwan's largest Traditional Chinese LLM research community with 1,000+ Discord members.
